How should I balance paying off student loans vs investing in retirement?
Great question! The optimal approach depends on several factors:
  • Your student loan interest rate (if >5%, prioritize paying these down)
  • Whether your employer offers retirement matching (if yes, contribute at least to the match)
  • Your emergency fund status (build this before aggressive loan repayment or investing)
A balanced approach might be: build a 3-month emergency fund, contribute to your 401(k) up to the employer match, pay down high-interest loans (>5%), then increase retirement contributions.
